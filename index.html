Pet Food Info

Basic recommended diets by pet type

Dogs

  • High-quality protein (chicken, beef, fish)
  • Wet vs dry: both fine — ensure proper hydration
  • Limit table scraps; avoid chocolate, grapes, onions

Cats

  • Obligate carnivores: protein-rich diets
  • Wet food helps prevent urinary issues; ensure taurine in diet
  • Avoid excessive carbs and dairy

Birds & Small Animals

  • Birds: seed mixes + fresh veggies; species-specific pellets
  • Rabbits: lots of hay, limited pellets, fresh greens
  • Hamsters: commercial mixes, fresh veggies occasionally

Pet Care Tips

Keys to a healthy, happy pet

Hygiene & Grooming

  • Brush regularly and bathe as needed for the species.
  • Trim nails, clean ears, and check teeth frequently.

Health Checks

  • Schedule vaccinations, deworming, and routine vet visits.
  • Watch for changes in appetite, energy, or behaviour.

Exercise & Enrichment

  • Daily walks for dogs, play sessions for cats, mental toys for birds.
  • Rotate toys and introduce training to keep pets engaged.

Safety & Comfort

  • Microchip and ID tag for outdoor pets; keep toxic plants/foods away.
  • Create a quiet safe space for rest and recovery.
